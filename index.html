Hope Alive Foundation

Restoring Hope to All

A humanitarian organization committed to dignity, care, and sustainable support for the elderly and less privileged in Nigeria.

About Us

Hope Alive Foundation is a non-governmental organization conceived as a vision over five years ago and brought to life at the end of 2025. We exist to restore hope, dignity, and care to elderly persons and underserved communities across Nigeria.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A society where every elderly person and less privileged individual lives with dignity, care, and renewed hope.

Our Mission

To restore hope and dignity by providing care, healthcare support, empowerment, and advocacy for the elderly and less privileged across Nigeria.

Our Programs & Impact

Food & Provisions

Distribution of foodstuffs and essential household provisions to elderly and vulnerable individuals.

Basic Healthcare

Blood pressure checks, malaria treatment, medication support, and mosquito net distribution.

Elderly Care Advocacy

Promoting dignity, inclusion, and awareness around elderly care and community responsibility.
